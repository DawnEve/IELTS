1.雅思阅读文章的来源有哪些？ 雅思A类文章有国外人文类、经济类和科学类的知名报纸、杂志或政府各部门(UK及世界各国) 的社会发展报告。 主要来自于学术出版物，而非一般娱乐性出版物。
New Scientist/The Economist/American Scientist/Scientific American/Science/Popular Science/Nature/Financial Times/Guardian/National Geographic/Discover/Time (Europe)/ Boston Global/History Today等期刊或杂志。
平时泛读时，可多选择以上报刊杂志中的内容，扩大相关背景知识，并了解雅思阅读的行文风格和常见结构。

[运营者工具箱] 单词计数( js版 )

Aim: 获得当天的英文格式的日期，和输入文本的单词总数。

Version: v1.0.2

Usage 1: (1)chrome中按F12打开console，aa=`输入英文文章`(数字键最左端的键)，回车; (2)复制平行线之间的代码到console，回车;

Usage 2: (1)chrome中按F12打开console，aa=`输入英文文章`(数字键最左端的键)，回车; (2)输入 ielts.calc(aa) 或者简写为 cp(`xxx`)，回车;

code: 

aa=`"The last time we looked at the polar ice sheets, Greenland was the dominant contributor. That's no longer the case."`


//1.字符串分割成数组
a2=aa.split(/[^a-zA-Z]+/) 
//(23) ["", "The", "last", "time", "we", "looked", "at", "the", "polar", "ice", "sheets", "Greenland", "was", "the", "dominant", "contributor", "That", "s", "no", "longer", "the", "case", ""]

//2.排除长度在2字母以内的数组元素
i=a2.length //数组长度
arr=[]
while(i>0){
	i-=1;
	if(a2[i].length>2) arr[arr.length]=a2[i];
}
arr
//(17) ["case", "the", "longer", "That", "contributor", "dominant", "the", "was", "Greenland", "sheets", "ice", "polar", "the", "looked", "time", "last", "The"]

//3.整理日期June 23, 2018
tmp=String(new Date()).split(/\s/)
//(7) ["Sat", "Jun", "23", "2018", "19:30:00", "GMT+0800", "(中国标准时间)"]

//4.输出结果
tmp[1]+" "+tmp[2]+", "+tmp[3]+' | '+arr.length+" words"


result:
"Jun 23, 2018 | 17 words"
